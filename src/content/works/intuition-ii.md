textEn: "Intuition: Untitled II is the second work in the Intuition series, following Intuition: Untitled I—an interactive sound installation made with sensors, copper, wires, and speakers. Where the first work draws attention to responsive currents and connection, this sculpture holds the same inquiry in a still, hand-drawn form.\n\nDrawn directly in space with a 3D pen, its porous ABS-filament structure recalls moss spreading across stones and the accumulated contours of rocks in the Icelandic mountains. Dense knots and open passages create a terrain that feels both delicate and geological."
